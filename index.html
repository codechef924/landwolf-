First Base Landwolf on the Base blockchain.


Base Landwolf, a charismatic and carefree wolf character form Matt Furie’s “Boy’s Club” comic series, is known for his laid-back attitude and affinity for fun.


As a friend of the internet sensation Brett the Frog, Base Landwolf share in the adventures and humor that have endeared the comic characters to fans worldwide.


Together with Brett and their other friends, Base Landwolf navigates the absurdity of life, leaving a trail of moments and laughter in his wake.


How to buy?

download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io

have ETH in your wallet to switch to $Bolf. If you don’t have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

connect to Uniswap. Go to app.uniswap.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $Bolf token address into Uniswap, select Brett, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

switch ETH for $Bolf. We have ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
